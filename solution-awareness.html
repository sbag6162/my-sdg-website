Awareness Campaigns

Our Awareness Campaigns shed light on inequality issues and encourage community engagement through:

  • Workshops & Seminars: Educating residents on social justice, gender equality, and civic rights.
  • Media Outreach: Partnering with local radio, newspapers, and social media influencers to amplify our message.
  • Community Events: Hosting inclusive festivals and marches that bring diverse groups together.

By fostering open dialogue and understanding, we aim to break down barriers and create a unified Motihari.
