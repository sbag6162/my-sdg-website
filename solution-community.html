Community Empowerment

Our Community Empowerment initiative focuses on creating opportunities for individuals to uplift themselves through:

  • Vocational Training: We partner with local NGOs to offer skill development workshops, ranging from carpentry to computer literacy.
  • Micro-Finance Support: Our micro-loan programs help small business owners and aspiring entrepreneurs get started.
  • Mentorship Networks: Experienced professionals guide participants in launching sustainable income-generating activities.

By empowering residents to become self-reliant, we reduce income disparity and foster inclusive growth. We’ve already reached over 500 families in Motihari through these community programs!
