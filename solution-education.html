Inclusive Education

Our Inclusive Education initiative tackles the education gap by:

  • Scholarships & Grants: Providing financial aid to underprivileged students from primary to tertiary levels.
  • Community Learning Centers: Setting up modern facilities with computers, libraries, and qualified teachers in rural areas.
  • Teacher Training: Equipping educators with updated curricula and innovative teaching methods.

By ensuring every child has access to quality education, we help break the cycle of poverty and empower future generations in Motihari.
